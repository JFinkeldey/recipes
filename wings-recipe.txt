Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds chicken wings
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 teaspoon red pepper sauce (or to taste)
 - flour
 - butter
 - 1/2 cup vinegar

Directions:
 1. Coat the wings in butter and flour
 2. Cook in 350 degree oven for 20 minutes
 3. Turn, then cook 20 more minutes
 4. Mix Vinegar and red pepper sauce
 5. Coat wings in vinegar and pepper sauce
